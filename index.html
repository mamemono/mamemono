— Our Story

A hobby.
A calling.
A cup for you.

"It started with one cup. Then another. Then an embarrassing number of failed roasts."

Mamemono — bean thing in Japanese — grew from a quiet obsession with specialty coffee and the warm, cozy world it creates. Born in Malaysia, shaped by Japanese café culture, and fuelled by a simple belief: great coffee should be accessible to everyone, not just those who can afford the premium tag.

We source from the finest origins — Ethiopia, Brazil, Colombia, Guatemala, and Indonesia — and roast every single bag fresh, on demand. No warehouses. No stale shelves. Just the bean, the roast, and you.

— Coffee Roast & Brew Guide

Grind size matters.
Here's how to brew it right.

Adjust grind size according to your brewing method for best flavour extraction. Our beans are roasted to medium-dark — rich, chocolatey, and versatile across all methods.

☕

Pour Over (V60 / Kalita)

Light to Medium Roast

Best for single origins — highlights fruity, floral, and tea-like notes. Medium-fine grind.

🫙

Aeropress / Chemex

Light to Medium Roast

Clean cup, bright acidity. Great for Ethiopia Washed Adado and Colombia Huila.

🍵

Espresso / Moka Pot

Medium-Dark Roast

Richer body, chocolatey, mild bitterness. Perfect for Ember and Terra house blends.

🫖

French Press / Drip Machine

Medium-Dark Roast

Full body, low acid. Works beautifully with Brazil Natural and Indonesia Mandheling.

🧊

Cold Brew

Light or Medium-Dark

Tea-like profile (light roast) or stronger, bolder cold brew (medium-dark). Both work.
